Welcome to KAP Hitters


KAP Hitters is a united cricket team formed by the proud communities of Kizhakumury and Pattery, coming together as one strong unit in 2023 during PPL Season 2. What started as a collaboration soon became a powerful symbol of friendship, sportsmanship, and unity within our region. From that moment forward, our journey continued through PPL Season 3 and PPL Season 4, growing stronger each year while carrying the spirit of togetherness from the East side of Perumpadappu. We are a team built on passion, dedication, and discipline. Our squad is filled with highly talented and hardworking players who consistently perform with heart and determination. Though we have faced challenges and haven‚Äôt yet reached the championship title we aim for, we believe success is not just about winning trophies but about the courage to keep fighting, improving, and supporting one another. Every match has strengthened our belief that teamwork, loyalty, and unity will lead us to victory. With the support of our community, fans, and well-wishers, this season we are ready to chase our dream harder than ever before. We step onto the field not just as players, but as representatives of our proud villages, carrying the hope and excitement of everyone who stands behind us.

This time, we will achieve it.
